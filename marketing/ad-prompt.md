# RxCompanion — 55-Second Cinematic Ad Prompt

Use this prompt directly in **Pippit AI**, **Runway**, or any AI video generator.

---

## Prompt

Generate a 55-second cinematic commercial advertisement for a digital medication safety platform called RxCompanion.

**Style:** Christopher Nolan thriller. Photorealistic. Dark to light emotional arc. No animation. No cartoon. No voiceover avatar. Real environments. Clinical and urgent in tone. Product reveal in second half only.

---

### Visual Progression — follow this exactly

Open on pure black for 2 seconds. Silence. Then slow fade into a dim kitchen at night. Cold blue lighting. A single hand holds a crumpled prescription paper under a lamp. The camera pushes in slowly toward the handwriting. Cut to a person standing completely still, staring at three different pill bottles on a counter. They do not move. Cut to an extreme close-up of a phone screen displaying only the words "Take twice daily." Cut to a hand hovering between two capsules, frozen mid-decision. The ticking of a clock builds underneath. Hard cut to black. White text appears centered on screen, large bold serif font:

> "46–77% of patients misinterpret their dosage instructions."

Text holds for 2 seconds. Fades. One more line appears:

> "They don't know they're doing it wrong."

Silence. Then a single clean digital tone. The screen floods with white light. A minimal, dark-mode web interface opens on a phone. The interface shows a drug interaction check completing with a green result. Then a dose calculator displaying a personalized result. Then a medication timer reading "Next dose in 8 hours." Each screen is shown for exactly 1 second. Everything feels fast, precise, controlled. Cut back to the same person from the opening — now calm, still, deliberate. They look at their phone. They take their medication correctly. The camera pulls back slowly. The scene is quiet and resolved. Final cut to a pure white background. The RxCompanion logo appears centered — blue and green, clean sans-serif. It holds for 3 seconds. Below the logo, text fades in:

> "Simplifying Prescriptions. Amplifying Health."

Slow fade to black.

---

### Cinematography Rules

- Slow push-in shots only
- Extreme close-ups on hands, screens, and objects
- No faces shown for more than 1 second
- No handheld movement
- No fast editing in the first half
- Hard cuts only between scenes
- Shallow depth of field throughout

---

### Lighting Rules

- First 28 seconds — cold blue, dim, high contrast shadows
- After product reveal — clean neutral white, bright, clinical precision
- The lighting shift IS the emotional shift

---

### Sound Design Rules

- Silence for first 2 seconds
- Then a slow single heartbeat once
- Then paper rustling
- Then low ticking that builds through scenes 2 to 4
- Hard silence at the black screen
- Single clean digital tone at product reveal
- Soft minimal ambient piano at 20% volume for second half
- Complete silence on the final logo card

---

### Text Overlay Rules

- White bold text, serif or clean sans-serif
- One line on screen at a time
- Never more than 8 words per line
- Center aligned
- Each text card holds for exactly 2 seconds before fading

---

### Duration

55 seconds exactly.

---

### Mood

> Tension that resolves into certainty.

---

### What Must Never Appear

- Cartoon visuals
- Stock footage aesthetic
- Smiling actors
- Pharmaceutical imagery (syringes, hospitals)
- Voiceover avatar
- Cluttered UI
- Fast-cut music video editing
